App Idea

Written By Abito Prakash Week 1

I am planning to develop an emergency service app. If a person needs blood urgently he can access this web app either for getting the contact numbers of donors in his/her area or for posting a blood request which will be notified to potential donors in the area. Also people who are willing to donate blood can see blood requests and can contact the one in need. Users can also sign up as donors, if they do they will be notified of future blood requests in their area. This is my app idea. I hope I can implement this :)
